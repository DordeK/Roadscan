RoadScan

Know what's coming before your suspension does.

Turn your phone into a road scanner. As you drive, RoadScan detects road surface problems, stores them on a shared live road map, and warns the next driver before they hit the same hole.

Why It Is A Problem

Europe has too much road damage to inspect by hand.

Potholes damage cars before cities know where the problem is.

Passive Road Scan

The phone silently builds the road map.

1
Drive normally. The app runs in the background using the phone sensors.
2
Detect road events. Sharp impacts are stored as potholes, bumps, or rough road sections.
3
Build the map. Each event is saved with GPS location, severity, confidence, and road surface type.
Advance Warning

The next driver gets an audio alert in advance.

1
RoadScan checks hazards nearby. The app fetches known potholes around the driver and compares them with current GPS position.
2
The warning plays at 50 meters. The alert is audio-first, so the driver does not need to look at the screen.
Main Technologies

Our stack

App

React Native

  • One mobile app for the driver.
  • Runs while driving.
  • Plays audio alerts.
Sensors

GPS + accelerometer

  • GPS tells us where the car is.
  • Accelerometer tells us what the road feels like.
  • Impacts become map points.
Detection

ML pothole model

  • Detects holes and bumps.
  • Measures severity from the impact.
  • Classifies road surface quality.
Map

Node.js + PostGIS

  • Stores all reports.
  • Merges repeated detections.
  • Serves nearby potholes for warnings.
Network Effect

The more people drive, the smarter the roads get.

1
driver detects a pothole. The first detection creates a warning point with location, severity, and confidence.
10
drivers confirm the same spot. RoadScan clusters the reports and raises confidence instead of creating duplicate pins.
1000
drivers map the city. Every commute becomes a passive road inspection, making the live road map more useful for everyone.
